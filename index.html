The system

Everything inside
your programme.

01 —

Individualised mesocycles

Every 5–6 weeks you receive a completely new training cycle built specifically for you — new exercise selection, fresh intensity targets, a clear theme. Strength. Hypertrophy. Athletic performance. Never a template.

02 —

Weekly progressive overload

Your exact sets, reps, and load targets are updated every single week based on what you actually logged the session before. The programme evolves with you — not on a fixed calendar.

03 —

Session intros — every workout

Every session opens with a written coach intro. Your mental cue, the focus of the day, a mindset principle, or a performance tip backed by research. You know exactly why you're doing what you're doing.

04 —

WhatsApp accountability

1–2 check-ins per week — real ones. Are you consistent? Are the weights feeling right? Is work taking over? Adjustments happen immediately, not at a scheduled call three weeks away.

05 —

Weekly form video reviews

Send your form clips. Get specific technical feedback — actual cues, actual corrections. Technique is the difference between progress and injury. This is non-negotiable in every package.

06 —

Lifestyle & nutrition guidance

Practical advice on sleep, stress, protein, energy management, and habits built around your real schedule. Not a meal plan — real guidance that fits into your life and makes everything else work better.

On lifestyle & nutrition guidance

This isn't a nutrition programme or a meal plan. It's the practical, evidence-informed advice that most coaches skip — the things that actually determine whether your training lands. Sleep quality and muscle recovery. Protein targets and timing without obsessing over macros. Managing energy around a demanding job. Keeping habits consistent when life gets busy. This is coaching for real people with real schedules.
